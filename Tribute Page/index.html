Jose de San Martin

El libertador de Argentina, Chile y Peru

A chronology of his life

  • 1778 - Born in Yapeyu, Corrientes
  • 1785 - Leaves Yapeyu to study in Malaga, Spain
  • 1808 - Serves on the spanish side of the peninsular war
  • 1811 - Resigns from the spanish army and moves to Argentina
  • 1812 - He marries Maria de los Remedios de Escalada
  • 1813 - The Battle of San Lorenzo is fought
  • 1816 - Argentina declares independence
  • 1817 - He crosses The Andes
  • 1818 - Chile declares independence
  • 1820 - He sails from Chile towards Peru
  • 1821 - He declares the independence of Peru
  • 1823 - He moves to Europe
  • 1848 - He settles on Boulogne-Sur-Mer
  • 1850 - He dies at 72 years old
